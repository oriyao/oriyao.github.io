Functions, Events & Parties

Public is a diverse venue with a variety of spaces on offer for functions, events and parties. We have two private dining rooms; The Library and The Dining Room, both can be used for private dining, whilst The Dining Room also offers boardroom capabilities.
The main restaurant and bar is also available for select full or partial venue hire.

Please email Anna at info@lovepublic.com.au for further information and availability

The Dining Room

A versatile space that can be used for both private dining and out of office board room meetings, this rooms sits 20 and also has the benefits of full connectivity for projected or amplified display. The dining room has also played host to movie nights, which we are able to seat up to 24 people for private viewings.

Award Winning

Winner of ”Best New Restaurant” and awarded “One Chefs Hat” Queensland Good Food Guide Awards 2012/2013

Winner of ‘Best Bar Food’ – Australian Bar Awards 2013/2014

Winner of 'Certificate of Excellence' 2014

Perched above the hustle of Brisbane’s CBD, on the first floor of 400 George Street, Public has burst onto the Brisbane dining scene with gusto since opening its doors in 2012.  Public offers an innovative and engaging dining experience with a clever, contemporary menu of share plates that are flawlessly executed.

Combining New Yorks sophistication with Brisbane’s warmth,  this venue has a number of different spaces including our main dining room, two private dining rooms and a welcoming bar.

Whether you drop by for a long lunch with colleagues, a romantic dinner for two, or simply treat yourself to cocktails in the bar; we’ve got you covered.
